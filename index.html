About Us

Sean Murphy

We're a team of recent graduates from Indiana University starting a web design & development agency. Our focus is primarily with small businesses and young entrepreneurs such as ourselves. Our mission is to grow the online presence of small businesses and entrepreneurs and give them an edge against their much larger competition at an affordable rate.
